Indian cuisine

Paneer tikka masala

Tender cubes of paneer enveloped in a luscious tomato-based sauce, infused with aromatic spices, offering a symphony of flavors.

Palak Paneer

Delicately simmered spinach infused with aromatic spices, embracing tender cubes of paneer, creating a verdant symphony of flavors and textures.

Rasgulla

Ethereal spheres of spongy cottage cheese, delicately poached in a fragrant syrup infused with cardamom, rose water, and saffron, offering a divine indulgence of sweetness and lightness.



Pani puri

Crispy hollow puris, filled with a tantalizing medley of spiced potato, tangy tamarind chutney, and fragrant mint-cilantro water, bursting with a symphony of flavors in a single exhilarating bite.

Masala dosa

A culinary masterpiece originating from South India, boasting a lacy golden crepe made from fermented rice and lentil batter, generously smeared with a savory masala of spiced potatoes, onions, and aromatic herbs, offering a harmonious blend of textures and flavors in every delectable bite.

Chole bhatura

A gastronomic delight hailing from the vibrant streets of North India, featuring fluffy, deep-fried bhaturas, paired with a sumptuous chole curry, brimming with tender chickpeas, infused with a melange of aromatic spices, delivering an exquisite blend of richness and spice that tantalizes the taste buds with every indulgent mouthful.
